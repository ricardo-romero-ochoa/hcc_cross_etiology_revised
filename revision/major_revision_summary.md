# Major revision summary

We substantially revised the analysis pipeline to address the reviewers' main concerns. The revised repository adds independent validation cohorts, dataset-selection criteria, explicit score definitions, module-size robustness analysis, meta-analysis heterogeneity statistics, TCGA-LIHC clinical/survival modeling, ESTIMATE-based adjustment, and a CIBERSORTx-adjusted regression pipeline for GSE121248.

The revised interpretation should avoid presenting HCCStateScore as an early-detection biomarker. It should be described as a bulk transcriptomic tumor-state score that captures the opposition between proliferation and hepatocyte functional loss in retrospective tumor/non-tumor tissue datasets.
